THE MISSION

Knowledge is the only effective weapon.

Why We Exist

In Baytown and across Texas, the relationship between landlord and tenant is fundamentally unbalanced. The system counts on your silence, your confusion, and your fear.

Most tenants don't realize that a simple phone call or a text message to a landlord regarding a repair has **zero legal weight** in a Texas courtroom. When a landlord ignores a broken AC in 100-degree heat or refuses to fix a lack of hot water, they are betting that you don't know the **Texas Property Code.**

"Stay Dangerous"

To "Stay Dangerous" doesn't mean violence. It means being **legally dangerous.** It means having a paper trail so tight that a landlord’s legal team can't find a single hole in your case.

We built this tool to automate the complex requirements of **Texas Property Code § 92.056.** This statute requires specific wording, specific delivery methods (Certified Mail), and specific timelines. If you miss one step, you lose your rights. We ensure you don't miss a single one.

The Baytown Standard

Our goal is to raise the standard of living for every renter in our community. When one tenant stands up using the proper legal channels, it makes it harder for slumlords to exploit the next person. We provide the tools; you provide the courage to use them.
