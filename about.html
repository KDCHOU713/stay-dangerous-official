The Reality

In the state of Texas, the law is often written in a way that favors those with the most resources. Landlords count on tenants being too busy, too tired, or too intimidated to look up their rights. They count on your silence to save them money.

The Philosophy

"Stay Dangerous" doesn't mean violence. It means being legally dangerous. It means when a landlord ignores a repair, you don't just complain—you send a Chapter 92 certified notice. It means when they threaten an illegal eviction, you show up with the code in your hand.
