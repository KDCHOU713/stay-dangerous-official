About the Stay Dangerous Generator

This platform was built to bridge the gap between complex legal codes and everyday people. The Stay Dangerous Habitability Generator is a specialized tool designed to help tenants formally document housing violations. Whether you are dealing with plumbing issues, electrical hazards, or structural concerns, our generator assists you in drafting a professional notice to your landlord.

How It Works

Under many state statutes, such as the Texas Property Code, a landlord's duty to repair is often triggered only after they receive a formal, written notice via certified mail. This tool automates that process, ensuring you use the correct terminology to protect your rights and create a verifiable paper trail.

Why Content Matters

Knowledge is power, but documentation is protection. By using this generator, you are taking the first step in self-advocacy. We provide the template; you provide the facts. Together, we ensure that no tenant has to live in sub-standard conditions due to a lack of resources.
