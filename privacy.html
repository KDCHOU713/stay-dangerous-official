Privacy Policy

1. Data Sovereignty

At Stay Dangerous, we believe your data is your business. Our Notice to Repair Generator is a client-side tool. This means the information you type—your name, address, and landlord details—is processed in your browser and used only to generate your PDF.

2. Information Collection

We do not store the personal details you enter into the legal forms on our servers. We use standard web analytics to see how many people are visiting the site, which helps us grow the mission.
